Frequently asked questions

Why is it compulsory to upload an ID to sign up?

This is to ensure the safety of all our users. Moreover, it is a mandate from the authorities for all space providers to collect a government issued ID from every user.

Do I have to show my ID at the space to get access?

Yes, you need to carry the same ID that was used to sign up. You may be denied access if you fail to produce the ID.

How can I earn credits?

You can earn credits by inviting others to join Nwook. Once your invitee creates an account using your invitation link, you both will receive credits worth 1 hour. You might need to logout of your account and log back in for the credits to appear in your account.

How can I use my credits?

You can use your credits at the time of payment after using a space. Please note that credits cannot be used for meeting room bookings and are applicable only on seat bookings at our Study Spaces. Also, credits are not broken down into minutes and maximum available credits are applied automatically.

Example 1:

Booking Duration: 40 Minutes

Credits Available: 2 Hours

Credits Auto Applied: 1 Hour

Example 2:

Booking Duration: 1 Hour 10 Minutes

Credits Available: 2 Hours

Credits Auto Applied: 2 Hours

Will I be charged for a cancelled booking?

No, we do not charge a cancellation fee at the moment.

What happens if I arrive later than my booking time?

Your booking will start at the chosen time and be valid for 30 minutes, post which it will be auto-cancelled if you do not arrive. However, you will be charged a no-show fee worth 1 hour which will be added to your next bill.

What happens if I arrive earlier than my booking time?

You can either choose to wait or check-in early. Your timer will start as soon as you check-in.

Will I be charged for the full hour if I stay for less than 60 minutes?

No, the charges will be prorated based on the time spent at the premises. However, if you redeem a free beverage, or use a Swimming Pool, you will be charged for the full hour.

How many beverages can I avail in an hour?

The number of beverages you can redeem in an hour is always equal to the number of seats you have availed. Please refer to the table below to know how beverage redemption works:

How many beverages are included in a meeting room booking?

The same as the capacity of the meeting room. Please note that by redeeming even 1 beverage, you agree to pay for the full hour as per the hourly rate of the meeting room.

What amenities or facilities are included in a meeting room?

At work spaces, a meeting room comes with a whiteboard and 1 beverage per seat. Please note that by redeeming even 1 beverage, you agree to pay for the full hour as per the hourly rate of the meeting room. Meeting rooms at study spaces don't come with a white board or beverages.

Can I redeem more than 1 beverage per hour?

The number of beverages you can redeem in an hour is always equal to the number of seats you have availed. You cannot redeem more than 1 beverage if you have booked only 1 seat. Also, please note that by redeeming a beverage, you agree to pay for the full hour. The duration will be rounded up to the next hour once you redeem a beverage. Can I buy beverages?

Yes you can.

Can I bring my own beverages and snacks?

Yes, you can carry beverages and dry snacks and consume them at your desk. However, food should only be consumed at designated areas or outside the premises. Alcoholic beverages are strictly prohibited.

Is smoking permitted?

Smoking is prohibited inside a space but some spaces do have designated smoking areas.

Can I carry alcohol?

Carrying alcohol to a space is strictly prohibited.

Can I bring in my visitors to the space?

Yes you can, but only after booking a seat for each visitor. You can do this through your account or ask the visitor to book one through the website.

If I book multiple seats or a meeting room from my account, do all my guests have to produce an ID?

No, only your ID will do.

Can I book a seat or a room on behalf of others?

No, others cannot access a space in your absence.

Can I use a space as a business or mailing address?

Yes you can. Please call our helpline on 080-395-14348 (Kolkata) or 080-304-74792 (Delhi) to know more.

Is storage space or locker facility available?

Certain spaces offer private lockers as storage space for personal belongings for a monthly fee.

Are pets allowed?

Pets are not allowed inside a space and there are no designated areas to leave your pets unattended.

How many beverages are included in a package?

Beverages are only included in packages for work spaces. The number of beverages is the same as the number of hours purchased.

Can I buy packages on behalf of others?

Yes you can. Please call our helpline on 9051875651 to know more.

Are packages transferrable?

No, a package can only be used by the registered user and is not transferrable.

Can I cancel a package after purchasing it? Will I get a refund?

Packages, once purchased, cannot be cancelled, returned or transferred. We cannot process any refunds for a package even if it hasn't been activated. However, you can choose to activate a package anytime after purchasing it and your validity will start only from the date of activation. The package will remain in your account unless you activate it.

Are card payments available?

No, we accept only cash or Paytm at the moment.
